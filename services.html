Our Services

Below are some of the services we offer. If you don't find something here, feel free to contact us to see if we can assist you!

Speech sound errors

Mistakes in producing sounds or words, sometimes caused by speech and language disorders, hearing impairments or learning a new language - e.g. replacing ("wabbit" for rabbit) or distorting sounds ("thip" for ship).

Therapy for children with special needs

We help children with a variety of diagnoses, intellectual disabilities and rare genetic disorders, including cerebral palsy, Down Syndrome, autism, and chromosome disorders.

Stuttering therapy

Stuttering is a speech disorder where sounds or words are repeated or prolonged. It's never a normal part of a child's speech development - treatment is tailored to age, goals, and other factors.

Literacy, reading & spelling

We help your child improve their reading ability, recognising letters and the sounds they make, along with writing, spelling and vocabulary building.

Therapy for adults & adolescents

Experience working with adults after a stroke, and with clients who experience dysphagia, aphasia and motor speech disorders.

Voice therapy

Therapy for voice disorders, teaching you how to use your voice correctly and how to care for it to prevent future issues.

Not sure what you need?

Get in touch and we'll help you figure out the right service for you or your child.
