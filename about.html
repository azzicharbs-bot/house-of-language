About Us

Personalised Telehealth speech pathology designed to help people communicate with greater confidence in everyday life.

How we work

Care shaped around the individual

House of Language provides online speech pathology for children, adolescents and adults. Support is available across articulation, language, literacy, stuttering, voice and social communication.

Every client has different strengths, goals and challenges. Sessions are personalised, evidence-informed and focused on practical communication skills that can be used beyond the appointment.
