About Us

Our mission is to help people improve their communication skills and reach their full potential. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to express themselves effectively, and we're here to make that a reality.

Our team of experienced speech therapists are passionate about what they do and are dedicated to providing the highest quality care to each and every one of our clients. They stay up-to-date with the latest research and techniques in speech therapy to ensure that our clients receive the most effective and cutting-edge treatment available.

We offer a wide range of speech therapy services, including but not limited to articulation therapy, language therapy, stuttering therapy, voice therapy, and social communication therapy. We work with individuals of all ages, from young children to seniors, and our therapists are experienced in treating a variety of speech and language disorders, including autism, cerebral palsy, and stroke.

At House of Language, we understand that every individual is unique, and that is why we take a personalised approach to each client. Our therapists will work closely with you to understand your needs, goals, and challenges, and then create a customised therapy plan that is tailored specifically to you.

We are proud to offer our speech therapy services in a Telehealth environment designed to make you feel comfortable and at ease. If you are looking for high-quality speech therapy services, we invite you to contact us and experience the difference that House of Language can make in your life.
